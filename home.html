My name is Jonas Olmstead. I write software for a living and in my spare time I make simple little video games. I also try to make big, complicated games but it usually ends in tears.

For the past couple of years I have tried entering game jams. I feel the hard deadline is very helpful in actually finishing a game. Therefore most of the games on my page were made in two or three days.

Do you like any of my games? Do you hate any of my games? Do you think one of my games could be great if only it had one more thing? Please feel free to mail me at "sanojo at hotmail.com"
